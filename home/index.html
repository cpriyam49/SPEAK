About

We can travel across borders and almost anywhere thanks to technology. With it, we are connecting people together, making millions of them more accessible, and fostering an inclusive and equitable society. There are approximately 466 million deaf persons in the world, and 80% of them are unable to understand the spoken and written languages of their respective nations, according to the World Health Organization. "Speak" strives to create new encounters and exchanges with hearing individuals in the community, whether at work, at school, or in families. It also aims to increase accessibility and inclusion for this population.

Through Speak, we hope to enable thousands of individuals throughout the world live better lives by assisting them in overcoming the still-very-visible communication barrier. We are now moving one step closer to the future, making the globe more open and Sign Language even more common.
